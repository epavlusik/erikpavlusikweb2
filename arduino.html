ARDUINO PROJECTS

What a great thing! I explored arduino and I think it was very good idea to make people more familiar with controlling electronic parts and make little projects. I keep ordering new parts and looking forward new projects that can solve little problems of daily life.

I present here two little projects I made and there are more to come soon.

PROJECT 1: Tea maker

The problem was that my lovely girlfriend used to forget the tea bag in the hot water and it made the tea a bit too bitter. I came with solution. Lets use arduino where she can set the time (for various types of teas there are various times) and the tea bag is pulled out of the cup.

PROJECT 2: Temperature and humidity measuring device

When I use to take long walks in Vienna, I really liked the old sundial with very beautiful classical decorations. I had vision to make dashboard that would show me the temperature and humidity and the background would show egyptian God of Sun and Poseidon.
