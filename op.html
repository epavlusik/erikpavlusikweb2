Older Projects

My first OOP applications were developed in C-sharp. My goal was to make application that can be helpful and solve real life problems, not just some meaningless uses of theory. I found very good book for learning C# by developing small games. This book explained very well the objects, classes, delegates, lambda expressions, interfaces etc.

Sure, first applications are never developed perfectly and there are some things which I would do differently now, but the best way to learn is simply do.

PROJECT 1: Wordtraining

I like to learn languages. What I dont like is having lists of words that can be lost easily. I created this program for me and my girlfriend in order to learn new words. It consist of feed that is loaded after appication is started and there are two modes: Learning mode and testing mode.

PROJECT 2: LifeTracker

This application is made to track what you eat, what sport activities are you practising and it gives you sort of points for the quality of the food (unhealthy food is red, healthy is green). You can then see your weeks and months and you have nice indicator of what kind of lifestyle do you have.

PROJECT 3: GreenHouseTool

This application is kind of accounting tool for farmer. He can track and see how much he earned.

PROJECT 4: Mealchooser

You open your fridge and have several indredients from which you want to coock some meal. You insert these ingredients in the app and the recepees including these ingredients are going to be listed. I enjoyed making this app a lot.

BONUS: Quantum Fly

I experimented with canvas element, had great book about HTML5 Games. So I was experimenting with it and see a great potential in it
