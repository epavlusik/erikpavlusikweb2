ART

My affaire with art started when I was able to hold pencil in my hand. I liked to paint, but I was not too serious with that until I have seen the paintings of Salvador Dali. My imagination exploded with ideas. I have piles of sketchbooks full of drawings.

The second big wave came, when I have seen the works of Alex Ross. This artist is in my opinon the most productive and hard working guy in his industry. It was Alex who inspired me to use gouache. It has one big advantage over the oil colour - it is water based medium, so it keeps my lungs clean.
